How to Use ScarletSniper
  1. Log in or create your account. Use your email and a password to sign up. If you already have an account, just log in.
  2. Enter your Rutgers NetID and password. This is needed so ScarletSniper can register you for classes on your behalf. Your information is kept secure and private.
  3. Type the index number of the course(s) you want to monitor. You can find the index number on the Rutgers course schedule. Enter it in the box on your dashboard (e.g. 12345).
  4. Click Add Courses. ScarletSniper will start monitoring those courses for you. You'll see them listed under "Active Monitors."
  5. Relax! ScarletSniper checks for open spots 24/7. If a spot opens, it will try to register you instantly. You'll get a notification if you're registered or if there's an issue.
  6. Manage your courses. You can stop monitoring a course at any time, or re-monitor a past request with one click.
  7. Upgrade if you need more slots. Free accounts can monitor one class at a time. Paid accounts can monitor moreâ€”just click "Buy More Slots" if you need to track additional courses.
If you ever need help, just use the contact info at the top of your dashboard. We're here for you!
